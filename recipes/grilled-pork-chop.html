Grilled Pork Chops

Easy to prepare with a simple marinade and cooks fast on the grill

With only a few ingredients these pork chops are both delicious and easy to put together with ingredients likely already in the kitchen.

It is a prep that can be put together in less than 5 minutes and cooked just as fast depending on how long you let it marinate.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 tbsp lemon pepper seasoning
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 6 pork loin chops

Steps

  1. Mix water, soy sauce, vegetable oil, lemon pepper seasoning, and garlic in bowl or bag, coat the pork chops thorougly and marinate 1-2 hours.
  2. Preheat grill to a medium-high heat of at least 350 degrees F.
  3. Grill pork chops until no longer pink with an internal temperature of at least 145 degrees F (about 5-6 minutes per side)
  4. Serve hot and enjoy.
